Welcome to the Hou Acupuncture Clinic

Are you seeking a holistic way to live a healthier and happier life with reduced pain and stress levels? At the Hou Acupuncture Clinic, we practice a 5000-year old medical system that diagnoses each person based on his or her constitution and treats the person, not the disease. Through clearing blockages, disharmonies and restoring balance in the body, we help people to regain their vitality and achieve their wellness goals.

Dr. Dayong Hou L.Ac., is committed to inspiring health and wellness in his patients, helping them attain optimal balance in mind, body and spirit. He strives to help patients lead healthier lives without pain or the dependency of drugs.

Common ailments treated include: Stress-Related Illnesses, Sinus Problems, Allergies, Sports Injuries, Back and Neck Pain, Headaches, Migraine, Weight Issues, Depression and Anxiety, Arthritis, Carpal Tunnel, Insomnia,

Each treatment is custom designed to meet each patient's individual needs and provide the utmost comfort and care to support the healing process. Depending on the patient's condition, treatments may include any of the following:

  • Acupuncture
  • Tui Na Massage
  • Cold Laser Therapy
  • Cupping
  • Moxibustion
  • Customized Herbal Formulas
